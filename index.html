Matrix Wizard 3000

To add a new matrix select from a drop-down list and click "+".
To remove a matrix click "X".
To change order of matrix click on "<" and ">".
To write your own figure click on "Start figure" and then click on canvas to add vertices. When you are done click "End figure" or "Cancel figure" to revert to default.
To test your knowledge click on "Add new target" and then try to find transformations used to create generated shape.
